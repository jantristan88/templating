Publication


It's amazing how 100 key words and phrases provide instant communication!

Do you want to speak simple Tagalog but are too busy to study it? Are you visiting the Philippines for a short time and want a Tagalog phrase book to help you communicate? If so, this is the book for you—it's the quickest and easiest way to learn the most common Filipino language. Its tiny 0.4 x 4.1 x 5.9 inches size makes it incredibly convenient to travel with but without losing the most essential content for communication.

The idea of Instant Tagalog is simple—learn 100 words and phrases and say 1,000 things. The trick is knowing which 100 words to learn, but the authors Jan Gaspi and Sining Marfori have solved the problem, choosing only those words you'll hear again and again. Even with a vocabulary this small, you'll be surprised how quickly and fluently you too can communicate in the Tagalog language. Added features include an easy-to-use pronunciation guide and Tagalog dictionary fore quick reference. Here's a sample of what you'll be able to do:

  • Meet people.
  • Go shopping.
  • Ask directions.
  • Ride the subway.
  • Order food and drinks.
  • And much more.

This illustrated dictionary presents the 1,500 key Tagalog words and sentences that speakers need to know to communicate in Tagalog. The handy format and highly visual presentation make it easy to learn and remember the words.

The dictionary is organized into 38 themes or topics. Each section presents 35-45 words with dozens of color photos, and several sentences to demonstrate the correct usage of the words. Every word and sentence appears in Tagalog along with the English meaning. This useful learning tool features vocabulary and photos that are specific to the Tagalog language and Filipino culture—and are not found in other Tagalog dictionaries.

Richly illustrated with over 750 color photographs, this useful Tagalog language learning tool includes a brief introduction to Tagalog pronunciation as well as an English-Tagalog index for quick reference. The free online audio files feature native Tagalog speakers reading all the vocabulary and sentences, so students can learn correct pronunciation!

If a picture is worth a thousand words, there's no more valuable tool for mastering Tagalog than the Tagalog Picture Dictionary. Whether users plan to visit the Philippines or are learning Tagalog abroad, this useful resource will help boost their proficiency quickly!
